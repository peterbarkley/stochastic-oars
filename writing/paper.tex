\abstract{We present a variant of matrix-parametrized proximal splitting allowing arbitrary coupling constraints over sub-elements of decision variables which permits decentralized execution over connected graphs.
These methods permit the extension of matrix-parametrized proximal splitting to multi-stage stochastic programming and other convex optimization problems with arbitrary coupling constraints over portions of the decision variables.
